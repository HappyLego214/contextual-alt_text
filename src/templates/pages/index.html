How It Works

1. Upload an image

Start by uploading the image you want to describe. Click the 'Upload Image' button and select an image from your computer or device. The tool supports various file types like JPEG, PNG, and more. Ensure the image is relevant to your content and of good quality for better analysis. Once uploaded, Cobalt’s AI will begin processing the image to understand its content and features.

2. Our AI analyzes the image

Once the image is uploaded, Cobalt’s advanced AI begins analyzing it. The AI identifies key features, objects, and context within the image, as well as understands the surrounding content you provide. This helps ensure that the generated alt-text is relevant and accurate.

3. You get alt text

After the analysis, Cobalt generates a detailed, context-aware alt-text description for your image. You can review, refine, or add specific keywords before using it on your report, website or content platform.

About Alt-Text

Alt-text is important for making your website accessible to people with visual impairments. It provides a description of images, allowing screen readers to share the image’s content with users who can’t see it. Without alt-text, many people would miss out on understanding your images.
Alt-text also helps with SEO. Search engines use it to understand what your images are about, which can improve your website’s ranking in search results. Cobalt makes it easy to create accurate, helpful alt-text, improving both accessibility and search visibility.
